### Cyber Security Task 2 – Future Interns (2026)

## 📌 Overview

This repository contains a Phishing Email Detection & Awareness Report developed as part of the Future Interns Cyber Security Program.

The project focuses on analyzing phishing email attacks and creating a user-friendly awareness guide to help individuals and organizations identify and avoid phishing threats.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze phishing email samples
* Identify common phishing indicators
* Classify email risk levels
* Explain how phishing attacks work
* Provide practical prevention and awareness guidelines

* **MXToolbox** – Header validation and inspection

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

### 🚨 Phishing Indicators Identified:

* Suspicious sender domain
* Urgency and fear-based language
* Malicious or misleading links
* Generic greetings (e.g., “Dear User”)
* Mismatch between sender and reply-to address
* Failed email authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)

---

## ⚠️ Risk Classification

* **Email Type:** Phishing
* **Risk Level:** High

The analyzed email contains multiple strong indicators of phishing, including header manipulation and domain spoofing.

---

## 🧠 How the Attack Works

Phishing attacks use social engineering techniques to trick users into trusting fake emails.

Users are pressured to act quickly, often leading them to click malicious links that redirect to fake websites designed to steal sensitive information such as login credentials.

---

## 🛡️ Prevention & Awareness Guidelines

### ✔ Best Practices:

* Verify sender email addresses
* Hover over links before clicking
* Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages
* Never share sensitive information via email

---

## ✅ Do’s and ❌ Don’ts

### ✅ Do:

* Verify emails before taking action
* Report suspicious emails
* Use strong passwords
* Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

### ❌ Don’t:

* Click unknown links
* Download unexpected attachments
* Respond to suspicious emails
* Share personal or login details
